Context

What we understand about the current situation

N2 has shifted into energy management and is still early in the market transition. The offer itself is compelling, but current outbound efforts have not yet produced enough traction or meeting volume.

The immediate goal is not to build a large multi-channel growth engine yet. The immediate goal is to generate consistent sales conversations through a properly managed outbound system and create the first layer of pipeline.

Investment model

How the commercial structure works

N2 owns the infrastructure. Ascendly builds and operates the outbound system on top of that infrastructure.

The client investment goes into the sending environment itself: domains, inboxes, sending tools, list sourcing, and campaign operations. Ascendly only charges for qualified attended meetings.

Notes

What the investment is designed to support

The smaller setup is designed to support a lower-cost pilot and create early proof. The larger setup is designed to support more consistent volume and a stronger monthly meeting range.

This proposal does not include the broader signal-based or website-visitor systems discussed conceptually. For now, this is a focused outbound effort built around current survival-stage priorities.

Next steps

How we move forward

1. Choose the path

Select either the 5–7 meeting pilot or the 10–12 meeting plan based on current cash availability and speed expectations.

2. Start setup

Once the infrastructure budget is approved, we begin buildout, prep the outbound system, and move into warm-up and launch.

This proposal is intentionally simple.

The goal here is not to overbuild. The goal is to get N2 into consistent sales conversations with a structure that keeps upfront risk lower and aligns cost with actual meeting delivery.
