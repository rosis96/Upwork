System

The three acquisition engines

Rather than relying on one channel, we build three coordinated engines that all feed the same sales pipeline.

1. Cold Outbound

Targeted list building, signal-based personalization, verified emails, and coordinated email + LinkedIn outreach.

  • Build targeted prospect lists
  • Enrich with real-world signals
  • Verify and protect deliverability
  • Run email and LinkedIn in parallel

2. Intent-Based Outreach

We prioritize companies already showing buying intent, including hiring, expansion, and growth signals.

  • Hiring for distribution or social roles
  • Music teams expanding operations
  • Agencies in growth or spending mode
  • Fast outreach while timing is hot

3. Visitor Capture

We identify qualified visitors landing on the site and trigger relevant outreach while interest is fresh.

  • Identify likely B2B visitors
  • Enrich and score automatically
  • Route by priority and fit
  • Launch outreach within minutes
Post-meeting

What happens after a meeting gets booked

Most outbound systems stop at booking. Ours keeps going. Every meeting becomes structured follow-up data so no opportunity goes cold from weak post-call execution.

AI-assisted follow-up

  • Meetings are recorded and transcribed automatically
  • AI extracts interest, objections, timing, and next steps
  • Follow-up drafts are created fast for review

Pipeline reactivation

  • Stale deals are monitored continuously
  • New signals trigger fresh re-engagement
  • Momentum stays alive beyond the first meeting
Deliverability

Why we do not launch on day one

Strong outbound depends on inbox reputation. That means we do not rush sending accounts live immediately. The first phase is infrastructure setup, warm-up, rotation planning, and testing.

The issue

Most agencies move too fast, over-send too early, and burn domains before momentum even starts.

Our approach

We warm properly, control send volume, rotate when needed, and protect long-term inbox performance.
