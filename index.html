Cold Outbound System

Email + LinkedIn running in parallel from day one

STEP 01Apollo.io
Build target list
Creators, brands, labels, agencies filtered by type, size and platform signals. 300–500 qualified prospects per batch. Buying signals included — recent hires, campaign launches, growth signals.
STEP 02Clay
Enrich with buying signals
LinkedIn profile, company data, recent content activity, job changes pulled per contact. Personalisation variable generated for each — the specific signal that makes line 1 of the email feel handwritten.
STEP 03Reoon
Verify every email
Invalid, catch-all and risky addresses removed before a single send. Domain reputation protected. Typically 70–85% of the list survives. This step protects the entire system.
STEP 04Instantly
3-step email sequence
Day 1: pain-led, 3 sentences, personalised first line, ends with a question. Day 3: follow-up with one insight. Day 7: breakup email. Instantly handles domain warming and inbox rotation automatically.
STEP 05Aimfox
LinkedIn 4-touch sequence
Runs in parallel with email. Connect request → personalised DM → value follow-up → soft ask for a call. Seeing outreach on two channels simultaneously increases response rate significantly.
STEP 06Loom + Make.com
Loom on warm signal
When a prospect shows intent (email open 3×, profile visit), Make.com triggers a 90-second personalised Loom showing their specific distribution gap. Converts at 3–5× plain text.

Workshop Funnel

One master playbook — any CDP runs the full funnel

STEP 01Typeform + Make.com
Registration page
Template landing page. CDP swaps date and vertical. Every submission auto-routed to Make.com. Clay enrichment runs on each registrant. High-score attendees get a personal pre-workshop message from the CDP.
STEP 02Instantly
Pre-workshop nurture
3 emails over 5 days. Email 1: welcome + "what's your biggest challenge?" Email 2: relevant insight or stat. Email 3: day-before reminder, "bring your current setup — we'll map it live."
STEP 03CDP delivers
Live workshop — distribution audit
60 minutes. CDP maps 2–3 attendee distribution setups live. Attendees see their own gap with numbers attached. The sale is half-closed before the offer is even made. Script and audit worksheet pre-built.
STEP 04Instantly
48-hour offer sequence
Email 1 (1hr after): soft offer — "we're taking 3 clients this month." Email 2 (24hrs): full offer + Calendly link. Email 3 (48hrs): deadline. Workshop attendees close at 3–5× cold outbound.
SCALESystem
CDP operability
4 CDPs running 1 workshop each per month = 4 independent funnels simultaneously. Revenue compounds across the system without central management. Reusable across every vertical.
OUTPUTCalendly
Strategy call booked
Interested attendees book directly via the Calendly link in Email 2 or 3. Booking triggers the full conversion system — pre-call brief generated, deal record created in Notion, CDP notified.

Lead Capture & Routing

Every source → one pipeline → under 30 seconds

STEP 01Make.com
All sources connected
Workshop registrations, website forms, LinkedIn replies, email replies, ad lead forms (Google, Meta, LinkedIn) — all fire to Make.com via webhook the moment a lead submits or replies. Zero delay.
STEP 02Clay
Enrich in real time
Company type, platform size, role seniority, recent launches — enriched per lead in under 5 seconds. A creator with 500k subscribers needs a different conversation than a brand with no creator relationships.
STEP 03Reoon
Verify instantly
Under 3 seconds. Valid → continue. Invalid → discard. CDPs never waste time on leads that can't be contacted. The full capture → enrich → verify → route cycle completes in under 30 seconds.
STEP 04Make.com logic
Score 1–10
Company type match (+3), role seniority: C-suite/VP/Head (+3), platform size: 100k+ or $50k+ ad spend (+2), growth signal: hire/funding/launch (+2). Score determines urgency and routing destination.
STEP 05Slack + Notion
Route to CDP with context
CDP receives Slack notification: lead name, company, role, ICP score, enrichment summary, suggested opening angle, Calendly link. Zero research required. CDP responds in minutes, not hours.
LIVENotion
Pipeline — shared live view
Every lead tracked from first touch. Source, stage, score, CDP assigned, last activity date. Stale for 5 days = auto-flagged and re-engagement triggered. All attribution tracked from day one.

Conversion System

From first reply to closed deal — nothing manual

STEP 01Instantly + Make.com
Calendly sent in <60 seconds
Positive reply detected by Instantly. Make.com fires. Calendly link auto-sent. A prospect who replies is in a window of intent — 2–4 hours. This system closes that window automatically every time.
STEP 02Make.com
Pre-call brief sent 1hr before
Prospect name, company, role, ICP score, pain signals, source, suggested opening question — all auto-generated and sent to CDP in Slack 1 hour before the call. CDP reads it in 3 minutes. Shows up prepared.
STEP 03Fathom
Call recorded + transcribed
Fathom auto-joins every Calendly call. Transcript ready 60 seconds after the call ends. Sent to Make.com via webhook. Pain level, interest level, objection, timeline, next step — all extracted automatically.
STEP 04Make.com + Instantly
Follow-up in 2 minutes
Written from the transcript. References what was discussed, the pain, the agreed next step. Appears in CDP Slack for one-click approval. Sent within 2 minutes of call end — 40% higher conversion than next-day sends.
STEP 05Make.com + Notion
Stale detection — daily 8am
Every open deal checked. No activity in 5 days = re-engagement message written (soft nudge, trigger-based, or social proof) and sent to CDP for one-click approval. No deal goes quiet by accident.
WINSystem
Closed → system continues
Deal Won: onboarding checklist auto-created. Deal Lost: archived, 90-day recheck set, Clay monitors for trigger events. A job change, funding round, or new launch fires a fresh re-engagement. No deal truly dies.
